Assetto Corsa Competizione

Assetto Corsa Competizione, the official GT World Challenge videogame. Thanks to the extraordinary quality of simulation, you will experience the atmosphere of the GT3 championship. Compete against official drivers, teams, cars and official circuits reproduced to the highest quality ever seen.

Intercontinental GT Pack

The Intercontinental GT Pack DLC introduces 4 iconic international circuits in Assetto Corsa Competizione, from four different continents: - Kyalami Grand Prix Circuit (South Africa) - Suzuka Circuit (Japan) - WeatherTech ® Raceway Laguna Seca (USA) - Mount Panorama Circuit (Australia) These tracks extend the GT Series to the Intercontin.

GT4 Pack

The GT4 PACK DLC for Assetto Corsa Competizione brings 11 new cars reproduced with maximum accuracy from the official GT4 European Series from SRO Motorsports Group. Get to the wheel and enjoy the purest GT4 action that KUNOS Simulazioni have ever developed, with Assetto Corsa Competizione.

2020 GT World Challenge Pack

The 2020 GT WORLD CHALLENGE PACK DLC adds a new track, two new cars, more than 60 new liveries and all teams of the 2020 GT World Challenge season in ACC, alongside with the new Pirelli 2020 "DHE" Tyre Model, new season BoP, improved AI algorithms and optimized overall performance.

British GT Pack

The BRITISH GT PACK DLC brings all the action of the 2019 British GT Championship by SRO Motorsports Group to Assetto Corsa Competizione and it includes 3 new circuits: Donington Park, Oulton Park and Snetterton. Furthermore , mixed class racing is included into the new version of ACC.

Challengers pack

The Challengers Pack for Assetto Corsa Competizione adds 5 new vehicles to the main game, including the all-new Audi R8 LMS GT3 evo II, which is set to be driven by former motorbike champion Valentino Rossi during next GT World Challenge season, 2022. The DLC also adds 4 more wonderful vehicles.

American Track Pack

The American Tracks Pack DLC for Assetto Corsa Competizione features 3 of the most iconic circuits of the United States of America. Circuit of the Americas, Indianapolis Motor Speedway and Watkins Glen International come to Assetto Corsa Competizione for the best US sim racing ever experienced!
